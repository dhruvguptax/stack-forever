Stack Forever

Stack the falling shapes as high as you can!
Reach the red dotted line to clear the level.

Controls:
- Click and drag the shape at the top to position it.
- Release the mouse to drop.
- During STRONG wind, click and HOLD blocks in your tower to stabilize them.

Tips:
- Color indicates weight (Darker = Heavier).
- Blocks touching for 3 seconds will glue together.
- Glue helps stability but can break in strong wind!
- Don't let any block fall off the screen!
